Wafi Trading Company

A Saudi company specialized in military industries seeks to be the main platform for providing military services and products to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and its allies with full specifications and international standards with Saudi competencies and expertise.

read more

our vision

To be among the first international companies specialized in military industries, equipped with the latest technologies and the most prominent national competencies, to work on developing military products and services with the highest international standards, and to work on self-sufficiency in the field of military industries for the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

our mission

We are working on developing the latest military technologies, manufacturing products with world-class standards and providing high-quality services to advance the military industries sector and working to secure the necessary supplies for our partners.

our aims

  • To be pioneers in developing and diversifying our work environment and employees in conjunction with the highest international standards
  • Building strategic companies with high reliability and long-term to enhance external security and national prosperity
  • Localizing military industries, highlighting strengths and focusing on them in our strategy, competencies, and manufacturing to provide the best defense solutions for our partners.
  • We manufacture high quality products and provide services at a competitive value
  • We will be among the first contributors to support the goal of nationalization from military spending by 50%, so that the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia will be self-sufficient
